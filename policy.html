Our Policy

Human Right Policy

Our hotel is working with strong family values, that means supporting vulnerable children and defend for their rights is our mission. Also, we are protecting our employees, and avoiding violence between them, sometimes our hotel also looks for employee situation, if they have any problems like financial problems, or mental illness problems, we are trying to solve this together. To encourage freedom of association and collective bargaining To offer fair and dignified work conditions and retribution Commitments to the environment and local communities To ensure promotion, distribution, visibility and public support

Oasis is committed to guaranteeing and respecting human rights, and avoiding labour exploitation, slavery, forced labour and child labour.
